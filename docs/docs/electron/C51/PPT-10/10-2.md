## 三角波

```asm
ORG 0000H
MAIN:
    MOV DPTR,#0000H
    MOV A.#00H 
UP:
    MOVX @DPTR,A 
    INC A
    JNZ UP
DOWN:
    DEC A 
    MOVX @DPPTR.A
    JNZ DOWN
    SJMP UP
    END
```
